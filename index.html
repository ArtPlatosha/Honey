Living well begins

Over its history as a food, the main uses of honey are in cooking, baking, dessers, as a spread on bread, as an addition to various beverages such as tea, and as a sweetener in some commercial beverages.

Due to its energy density, honey is an important food for virtually all hunter-gatherer cultures in warm climates, with the Hadza people naking honey as their favourite food.
